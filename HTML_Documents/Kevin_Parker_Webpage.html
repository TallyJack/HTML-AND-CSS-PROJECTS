This is Kevin Parker, aka Tame Impala

Although Tame Impala technically refers to an "artistic project," which is partially a collaborative performance with touring band members, the lead performer, writer, and producer of all of Tame Impala's music is Kevin Parker.
Therefore, it would not be wholly inaccurate to say that Tame Impala is essentially one man, often accompanied by other bandmates during live performances.

"If my take-off fails, make up some other story, and if I never come back, tell my mother I'm sorry."
